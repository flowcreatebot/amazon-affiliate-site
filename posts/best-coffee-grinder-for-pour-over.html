Best Coffee Grinder for Pour Over: What Actually Matters

For pour over, grind consistency matters more than sheer motor power. Better particle distribution means cleaner flavor and better sweetness.

Buying checklist

  • Burr type: Conical is forgiving, flat burr often gives higher clarity.
  • Retention: Lower retention means less stale grounds contaminating your next cup.
  • Workflow: Static, dosing cup fit, and noise affect daily usability more than spec-sheet RPM.

Common mistake

Buying a cheap blade grinder and trying to “pulse your way” to consistency. It causes uneven extraction and muddy cups. A basic burr grinder is a much better first upgrade.
